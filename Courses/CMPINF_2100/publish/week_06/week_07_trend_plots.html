Trend Plots¶

We can add trend lines to visualize the LINEAR relationship between two continuous variables.

TREND LINES correspond to the BEST FIT LINE between two variables.

Later on..we will learn HOW this line is created..but for now lets visualize it and let the line or TREND HELP US interpret the relationship!

The TREND is not crated with the sns.relplot() function.

Instead, the TREND is created with the FIGURE LEVEL function...sns.lmplot().

In [8]:
sns.lmplot(data=penguins,
           x="flipper_length_mm",
           y="bill_depth_mm")
plt.show()
